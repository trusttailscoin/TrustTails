Roadmap

Honest Phases, No False Promises

Most crypto roadmaps read like marketing copy — vague milestones, confident timelines, no accountability. This one works differently. Every item is labelled with its real status: live, exploratory, or long-term unconfirmed. We'd rather tell you what we don't know than imply certainty we don't have.

Transparency note

Why We Word It This Way

Language in crypto carries legal weight. When a project says "we will partner with X" or "listing confirmed Q2," those words can constitute representations to investors — and they create expectations that, if unmet, damage trust or invite regulatory scrutiny.

Live = Done

If we say something is live, it is verifiable on-chain or linkable right now. No "soft launches," no "in testing" dressed up as complete.

Exploratory = Intention

Exploratory phases describe what we are actively thinking about and researching — not what we have committed to deliver by a specific date.

Unconfirmed = Aspiration

Long-term directions are aspirations, not announcements. We state them so you understand the vision — not because they are agreed or guaranteed. See our Vision page for more.

Three Phases

Where We Are and Where We're Headed

Each phase has a distinct character: proof of existence, proof of utility, and a long-term north star that remains explicitly unconfirmed.

Phase 1 — Live Now

Community Foundation

Phase 1 is about establishing a verifiable, honest foundation. The thesis is simple: a meme coin that earns attention through transparency rather than hype. Everything listed here is complete and independently verifiable.

  • Token created on Solana (SPL). Total supply: exactly 1,000,000,000 TAIL. Fixed forever — no inflation mechanism exists. See tokenomics.
  • Mint authority revoked. No new TAIL can ever be created. Verifiable at Solscan.
  • Freeze authority revoked. No wallet can ever be frozen by the team. Your tokens are yours.
  • Website live with full transparency pages: Is it legit?, Transparency, Security.
  • Whitepaper published. Download whitepaper v1 (PDF) or read online. No claims are made that cannot be substantiated.
  • Community channels open: X @trusttailscoin, Telegram community, Telegram announcements.
  • Pre-launch status: TAIL is not yet trading. There is no Raydium pool, no DexScreener listing. Be alert — any account claiming to sell TAIL right now is a scam. We will never DM you first about a presale.
Phase 2 — Exploratory

Independent Infrastructure

Phase 2 is an intention, not a scheduled delivery. It describes what we are actively thinking about building — the kinds of capabilities that would make TAIL useful rather than merely speculative. No delivery date is attached because we do not yet have one.

  • Lower, predictable transaction fees. One of the most persistent friction points in crypto payments is fee unpredictability. We are exploring whether TAIL can be structured to offer more consistent cost-to-transfer for its use cases — without relying on third-party fee-waiver claims we cannot control.
  • Speed and reliability improvements. Solana already offers fast finality. We are researching whether there are additional infrastructure or tooling choices that further improve reliability for practical use — particularly if payment-adjacent use cases develop.
  • Optional compliance features. If TAIL moves toward finance-adjacent utility, some participants may need on-chain records compatible with their own reporting requirements. We are exploring what optional, privacy-respecting tooling might look like — noting that this remains highly uncertain.
  • Exchange and liquidity groundwork. Phase 2 is where we explore public trading readiness: DEX pool creation (most likely Raydium on Solana), and possibly CEX conversations. Nothing is agreed. We will announce when something is confirmed, not before.

Why no dates? Because we will not set a date we are not confident we can meet. Announcing "Q3 DEX listing" and missing it is a common community trust-breaker. We'd rather under-promise and over-deliver.

Phase 3 — Long-term, Unconfirmed

Finance-Sector North Star

Important: TrustTails has no current partnership, affiliation, or agreement with any bank, financial institution, payments network, or regulated entity. None. This phase describes an aspiration we hold — not a reality, not a negotiation in progress, not a rumour with substance.

The long-term thesis is that a token which earns credibility through radical transparency and community trust — rather than through marketing — might eventually become relevant in finance-adjacent contexts. What that could look like:

  • Utility in cross-border payment corridors where low, predictable fees and fast settlement have genuine value.
  • Potential for institutional or semi-institutional parties to explore TAIL as a settlement or collateral layer — if and only if the regulatory environment, token behaviour, and community governance mature enough to support it.
  • Conversations with regulated entities about what responsible, transparent token infrastructure looks like — with the recognition that such conversations are long, complex, and frequently go nowhere.

We describe this phase because we believe in the direction. We label it "unconfirmed" because integrity requires it. If a partnership or institutional agreement is ever reached, we will announce it publicly and verifiably — not through a rumour campaign or a teased logo. Read more about the longer-term thinking in our Vision and Whitepaper.

How we communicate

What to Expect from TrustTails Updates

Knowing how we will and won't communicate helps you identify scams and manage expectations. These are firm commitments about our communication style.

We will

  • Announce confirmed developments on @trusttailscoin and Telegram Announcements
  • Label exploratory items as exploratory and unconfirmed items as unconfirmed — in writing, publicly
  • Update this roadmap when the status of any item changes
  • Provide on-chain verification links for every claim that has one — always verify on Solscan

We will never

  • DM you about a presale, early access, or whitelist — these are always scams
  • Name a partner before an agreement is signed and publicly verified
  • Set a roadmap delivery date we are not confident we can meet
  • Imply price targets, investment returns, or "guaranteed" outcomes of any kind
Common questions

Roadmap FAQ

When will TAIL be available to buy?
TrustTails is pre-launch. There is no active trading pool and no confirmed listing date. When a DEX pool goes live, it will be announced on @trusttailscoin and Telegram Announcements with verifiable on-chain links. Any account or message claiming you can buy TAIL right now is a scam.
Is Phase 3 a real partnership with a bank or payments company?
No. TrustTails has no current partnership, affiliation, or agreement with any financial institution, payments network, or regulated entity. Phase 3 describes a long-term directional aspiration — the kind of relevance we believe is worth building toward. It is explicitly unconfirmed. If any such agreement ever materialises, we will announce it publicly with verifiable documentation.
Why is Phase 2 exploratory rather than committed?
Because we have not yet committed to a specific technical architecture, delivery timeline, or set of partners for Phase 2 infrastructure. Labelling it "exploratory" is the accurate description of where we are. Setting confident dates and then missing them is one of the most common ways crypto projects destroy community trust. We prefer to be honest about uncertainty up front.
Can the total supply ever increase?
No. The mint authority has been revoked, which means no new TAIL can ever be created. The total supply is permanently fixed at 1,000,000,000 TAIL. You can verify this directly on Solscan — the mint authority field will show "null" or revoked. See our Tokenomics page for a full breakdown.
How will I know if the roadmap changes?
This page will be updated. We will also post changes to @trusttailscoin and the announcements channel. Follow both to stay informed. We will not bury changes in fine print — if a phase is delayed, deprioritised, or removed, we will say so plainly.
Is this financial advice?
No. Nothing on this website, in our whitepaper, or in our community channels constitutes financial advice or an investment recommendation. Cryptocurrency — including TAIL — can lose all of its value. Make your own decisions, preferably with guidance from a qualified financial adviser who understands your personal situation.
