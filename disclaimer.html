Market Volatility

Cryptocurrency markets, and especially early-stage community tokens, experience extreme price swings driven by sentiment, speculation, and macroeconomic factors entirely unrelated to any project fundamentals. Past price data, where it exists, is not predictive of future performance.

Verify Everything On-Chain
