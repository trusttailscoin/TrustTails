Step by step

How Buying TAIL Will Work

These steps reflect how a typical Solana token purchase works. Prepare now so you are ready on launch day — and so you recognise any deviations from this process as potential red flags.

Do this now

Step 1 — Set Up a Solana Wallet

You need a non-custodial Solana wallet to hold and swap TAIL. The two most widely used options are:

  • Phantom — browser extension and mobile app, beginner-friendly. Download only from phantom.app.
  • Solflare — browser extension and mobile app, well-regarded in the Solana ecosystem. Download only from solflare.com.

During setup, you will be given a 12- or 24-word seed phrase. Write it on paper. Store it offline. Never type it into any website or share it with anyone — not even someone claiming to be TrustTails support. There is no such thing as legitimate "wallet verification" that requires your seed phrase.

Do this now

Step 2 — Fund Your Wallet with SOL

TAIL will trade against SOL (Solana's native token). You will need SOL in your wallet for two purposes:

  • To swap for TAIL once it is live on a DEX.
  • To pay Solana network transaction fees (called "gas"), which are typically a fraction of a cent per transaction.

You can purchase SOL on regulated centralised exchanges (such as Coinbase, Kraken, or Binance, depending on your country) and then withdraw it to your Phantom or Solflare wallet address. Make sure you withdraw on the Solana network — not ERC-20 or any other chain. Only invest what you can afford to lose entirely. Cryptocurrency, including SOL, carries significant risk.

Step 4 — Swap SOL for TAIL on a Solana DEX

Once TAIL is live, you will be able to swap using a Solana-native decentralised exchange aggregator. The two most commonly used are:

  • Jupiter (jup.ag) — aggregates liquidity across Solana DEXes for best pricing.
  • Raydium (raydium.io) — one of Solana's primary AMM pools; TAIL may be listed here directly.

When you open either platform, paste the official TAIL mint address — 4NoNV3jSYLRbUtVWSTK5XdkpuvRzGpMCmfZSBKMuk6Rc — into the token search box. Do not search by name alone; scam tokens intentionally copy project names. Always verify the address matches before confirming any swap.

Connect your wallet, enter the amount of SOL you want to swap, review the transaction details, and confirm. Your TAIL tokens will appear in your wallet once the transaction is processed on-chain, typically within seconds.

After purchase

After swapping, you can independently confirm your TAIL balance by visiting Solscan and entering your wallet address, or by viewing your wallet's token list directly in Phantom or Solflare.
