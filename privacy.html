Legal

Privacy Policy

TrustTails is an informational static website. We collect very little data, we do not sell it, and we are straightforward about what happens when you visit.

Last updated: June 2026. This policy applies to trusttails.io and its subpages. For our broader terms of use, see the Terms & Conditions. For risk disclosures specific to the TAIL token, see the Disclaimer.

Data We Collect

Minimal by design

This site has no user accounts, no login forms, no purchase flows, and no advertising network. There is very little information we could collect even if we wanted to.

Server log data

When your browser requests a page, our web host automatically records the page URL, your browser type, referring URL, and IP address. This is standard HTTP access-log behaviour. Logs are retained for a short period for security and debugging purposes only, then discarded.

Analytics (if active)

We may use a privacy-respecting analytics tool (such as a self-hosted or cookieless solution) to understand aggregate page traffic — for example, which pages are visited most. If we do, no personally identifiable profiles are built and no data is sold or shared with advertisers.

Email contact

If you email us at trusttailscoin@gmail.com, we receive your email address and the content of your message. We use this only to respond to your enquiry. We do not add you to mailing lists without your explicit consent.

Use & Retention

How we use the limited data we have

We use data to

  • Keep the site running and diagnose technical errors
  • Understand aggregate visitor traffic to improve content
  • Respond to direct email enquiries you initiate
  • Detect and block abusive traffic or security threats

We do not

  • Sell, rent, or trade your data to any third party
  • Build advertising profiles or retarget you with ads
  • Share data with data brokers or marketing platforms
  • Require an account, login, or personal registration
Cookies & External Links

Cookies and third-party services

Cookies

This site does not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies. Our web hosting provider may set a minimal session or security cookie as part of serving content — this is technical infrastructure only and does not track you across other websites. If you use an analytics-blocking browser extension, it will not affect your ability to read this site.

External links

This site links to third-party platforms including X (Twitter), Telegram, and Solscan. Each of those platforms operates under its own privacy policy. We are not responsible for their data practices. Please review their policies before sharing personal information there.

Important Notes

Children, your rights, and scam warnings

Children's privacy

This site is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a child has submitted personal information via email, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (including the EU/UK under GDPR and California under CCPA), you may have the right to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold about you. Because we hold almost no personal data, most requests can be resolved quickly. Email trusttailscoin@gmail.com with your request and we will respond within 30 days.

Scam warning

TrustTails is pre-launch. We will never DM you first on Telegram or X, request your wallet seed phrase, or run a private presale. Any account claiming to offer early TAIL tokens or asking for wallet access is a scam. Our only official channels are listed on the Community page and in the Is it legit? guide.

Quick Answers

Privacy FAQ

Do you use Google Analytics or similar tracking?
Not at launch. If we ever introduce an analytics tool, we will update this policy and preference cookieless, privacy-preserving solutions. We will never install advertising-network pixels (e.g. Meta Pixel, Google Ads remarketing tags) on this site.
Can I read TrustTails without giving you any information?
Yes. The entire site is publicly readable. You do not need to register, log in, or submit any form. Your IP address is logged by our host's server infrastructure as with any website, but no account or profile is created.
What happens if I join the Telegram community?
Joining t.me/TrustTailsCommunity or t.me/TrustTailsOfficial means you are using Telegram's platform under Telegram's privacy policy. We can see your Telegram display name and messages you post publicly in the group. We cannot access your phone number, private chats with other users, or any information Telegram holds privately. Review Telegram's privacy policy for full details.
Will this policy change?
We may update this policy if the site adds new features (e.g. a newsletter, a community portal). When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, for material changes, post a notice in our Telegram announcement channel. Continued use of the site after a policy update constitutes acceptance of the revised terms.
Is visiting this site financial advice?
No. Nothing on trusttails.io constitutes financial or investment advice. TAIL is a speculative token that could lose all of its value. Please read our full Disclaimer and do your own research before making any financial decisions. Always verify on-chain data independently via Solscan.
Questions?

Contact us directly

If you have a privacy question, a data request, or just want to verify something, email us. We read every message and aim to respond within 30 days. You can also review our Terms & Conditions and Transparency page for more on how TrustTails operates.
