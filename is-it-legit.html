Honest Accounting

What Is True — and What We Never Claim

The clearest sign of a credible project is knowing exactly what it can and cannot prove. This is our line.

What Is Verifiable Right Now

  • Fixed supply of 1,000,000,000 TAIL. The total token count is locked on-chain and publicly readable. No team wallet, no treasury, no reserve can mint more.
  • Mint authority is revoked. The ability to create new TAIL tokens has been permanently disabled. This is a one-way, irreversible action on Solana.
  • Freeze authority is revoked. No account — including the original deployer — can freeze, restrict, or confiscate a holder's tokens.
  • Public contract address. The mint address is published here, in the whitepaper, and on every official channel. One address. Cross-check it.
  • Whitepaper is publicly available. Read or download the full whitepaper — our thesis, tokenomics, and roadmap are written down, not whispered in Discord.
  • Pre-launch status is honest. TAIL is not yet trading. There is no Raydium pool, no DexScreener listing. We say this plainly rather than quietly starting a pool and hoping nobody notices.

What We Do Not Claim

  • No price predictions. We will never tell you where the price is going. Anyone who does is guessing or selling.
  • No security audit (yet). We have not completed a formal third-party security audit. When one is completed, it will be published with a direct link to the auditor's report.
  • No institutional partnerships. TrustTails has no current affiliation, partnership, or endorsement from any bank, payments company, or financial institution. Phase 3 finance-sector relevance is an unconfirmed long-term aspiration, not a current reality.
  • No exchange listings confirmed. Any claim of a confirmed CEX or DEX listing before we announce it officially is false.
  • No presale. There is no presale, private sale, or early-access token offering. If someone offers you TAIL at a "presale price" via DM, that is a scam.
  • No guarantees of any kind. Crypto can lose all its value. TAIL is not a savings product, investment vehicle, or guaranteed return. Please only put in what you can afford to lose entirely.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Is TrustTails a rug pull risk?

The two main on-chain rug vectors for SPL tokens are (1) minting new supply to dump on holders, and (2) freezing holder wallets. Both require mint and freeze authority. TrustTails has revoked both — permanently and verifiably. You can confirm this on Solscan right now. That does not eliminate all risk in a speculative pre-launch token, but it removes two of the most common mechanisms. See our security page for more detail on what this means technically.

Has TrustTails been audited?

No formal third-party security audit has been completed yet. We will not claim one has been done when it has not. When an audit is completed, we will publish the full report with a link to the auditor's own website — not a screenshot, not a summary. Until then, the verifiable protections are the revoked authorities described on this page.

Why should I trust a meme coin?

You should not trust any token based solely on branding. TrustTails started as a meme — that is part of the story, not something we hide. The thesis is that attention earned through community can be converted into something more durable over time, but that has to be demonstrated phase by phase, not promised upfront. The on-chain facts are a baseline, not a full endorsement. Read the about page for the full picture of where we are starting from and where we are trying to go.

Where can I buy TAIL?

TAIL is pre-launch. It is not yet available to buy on any DEX or CEX. There is no pool on Raydium, no DexScreener listing, and no presale. When the public launch happens, it will be announced through our official X account and Telegram announcements channel. Anyone claiming to sell you TAIL right now is running a scam.

Is TrustTails affiliated with any bank or financial institution?

No. TrustTails has no current affiliation, partnership, endorsement, or agreement with any bank, payments processor, or financial institution. The Phase 3 vision of finance-sector relevance is an explicitly unconfirmed long-term direction. No such relationship exists today. If one is ever established, it will be announced publicly — not hinted at in copy.

How do I report a fake TrustTails account or scam?

Email us at trusttailscoin@gmail.com or flag it in the official Telegram community. Report the fake account directly to the platform it appears on. Scam accounts typically impersonate team members, claim insider access, or create urgency around a presale. None of those patterns come from us.
