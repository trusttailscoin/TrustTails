What Is TrustTails (TAIL)?
A Plain-English Introduction

TrustTails is a community-first token on Solana with a fixed supply and revoked authorities. Here is everything you need to know — honestly, without the hype.

The basics

The One-Paragraph Answer

TrustTails (ticker: TAIL) is a token built on the Solana blockchain. It has a fixed total supply of exactly 1,000,000,000 TAIL — not a single token can ever be created beyond that. The mint authority and freeze authority have both been permanently revoked, which means no developer, team member, or anyone else can inflate the supply or freeze your wallet. Those decisions are done, recorded on-chain, and irreversible.

It started as a meme. The team is candid about that. But the project's thesis is that attention earned through a meme is only valuable if what comes next is built with genuine care. Everything after the name has to be earned, not claimed.

Current status: Pre-launch

As of now, TAIL is not yet trading. There is no Raydium pool, no DexScreener listing, and no presale. If you see anyone claiming otherwise — in a DM, a Telegram group, or anywhere else — treat it as a scam. The only official channels are listed at the bottom of every page on this site.

You can verify the token on-chain right now at Solscan. The mint address is 4NoNV3jSYLRbUtVWSTK5XdkpuvRzGpMCmfZSBKMuk6Rc. Checking that yourself takes thirty seconds and costs nothing.

Philosophy

Why "TrustTails"? What the Name Actually Means

The name is two words doing two jobs at once.

Trust

Not claimed upfront, and not something a white paper alone can give you. In the context of crypto — a space full of rug pulls and empty promises — trust is something that has to be demonstrated slowly, through consistent and verifiable action over time.

Tails

A nod to the meme origin — a coin flip, a coin toss, the back of a coin. It is also a signal that the team is not pretending to be something polished from day one. Tails is the honest side: less glamorous, more real.

The Direction

Together, TrustTails points at a long-term ambition: to build credibility in a sector that does not often earn it. The project's stated long-term direction involves relevance to the finance sector — but that is explicitly unconfirmed and distant. It is a direction, not a delivery date.

Under the hood

What "Revoked Authorities" Actually Means for You

These two words get used a lot in crypto. Here is what they mean in plain English for anyone holding or considering TAIL.

Mint authority revoked means...

  • Nobody can ever create additional TAIL tokens
  • The 1,000,000,000 supply is the ceiling, permanently
  • The team cannot secretly print tokens for themselves later
  • This is verifiable on-chain — you do not have to take our word for it

Freeze authority revoked means...

  • Nobody can lock or freeze your TAIL wallet balance
  • The team cannot block transfers or make your tokens inaccessible
  • Once you hold TAIL, your access to it is yours alone
  • Again: verifiable on-chain, not a promise from us

Stay safe

Pre-Launch Warning: Scams Target This Space

What scammers do

Because TrustTails is pre-launch, there is no way to buy it yet. That gap is exactly what scammers exploit. Common tactics include:

  • Fake "presale" links sent via DM on Telegram or X
  • Impersonator accounts using similar names or logos
  • Unofficial Telegram groups mimicking the real community
  • False claims of a Raydium or DexScreener listing

Official channels only

The only legitimate TrustTails presence is:

  • X: x.com/trusttailscoin
  • Community Telegram: t.me/TrustTailsCommunity
  • Announcements: t.me/TrustTailsOfficial
  • Email: trusttailscoin@gmail.com

Nobody from TrustTails will ever DM you first with a buy link or presale opportunity.

Common questions

Quick Answers for Newcomers

Is TrustTails a scam?

It is a fair question to ask. The team has made specific, verifiable claims: fixed supply of 1,000,000,000 TAIL, mint authority revoked, freeze authority revoked, all verifiable at Solscan. The project does not promise returns, partnerships, or a timeline. The Is it legit? page walks through the key red flags to check for — in this project and in any crypto project.

Can I buy TAIL right now?

No. TrustTails is pre-launch and not yet trading on any exchange or decentralised exchange. There is no presale. When a public launch occurs, it will be announced through official channels only. If anyone tells you there is a way to buy now, that is a scam.

What blockchain is TAIL on?

TrustTails is an SPL token on Solana. Solana was chosen for its transaction speed and low fees. You can read more about that choice on the Why Solana? page.

Who created TrustTails?

The project is community-first and does not rely on a named founder as a central trust signal. The About page covers the project's background and philosophy. The team's view is that what matters is verifiable on-chain data and consistent behaviour over time — not a face attached to a white paper.

Please do your own research and consider speaking with a qualified financial adviser before making any decisions. The whitepaper contains the full disclaimer.
