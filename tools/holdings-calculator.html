Explainer

How this calculator works

The maths is deliberately simple — and that simplicity is the whole point.

Step 1 — Enter a quantity

Type how many tokens you hold or plan to hold. For TAIL the maximum possible quantity is the total supply: 1,000,000,000. Your allocation will be a fraction of that.

Step 2 — Choose a hypothetical price

Pick any USD price per token to test. You can enter $0.00001, $0.001, $0.01, or any other figure. None of these are forecasts. They are numbers you choose to run through arithmetic.

Step 3 — Read the result

The output is tokens × price. That is all. The calculator cannot predict whether any price will ever be reached. Markets are determined by supply, demand, liquidity, and countless other factors no calculator can model.

Key terms defined

Token holdings

The number of a particular crypto token you own. For TAIL, holdings are recorded on the Solana blockchain and can be verified via your wallet address on Solscan.

Hypothetical value

What your holdings would be worth if a token traded at a specific price. It is arithmetic — not a forecast, not a guaranteed outcome, and not a solicitation to buy.

Market capitalisation

Price per token × circulating (or total) supply. For TAIL, total and circulating supply are identical: 1,000,000,000 — the mint authority is permanently revoked so no new tokens can ever be created. Market cap calculator →

Fixed supply

TAIL has a hard cap of 1,000,000,000 tokens. No inflation, no additional minting. The mint authority has been revoked and can be verified on-chain at any time via the contract address.

SPL Token

A standard token on the Solana blockchain (analogous to ERC-20 on Ethereum). TAIL is an SPL token, meaning it runs on Solana's fast, low-fee infrastructure and can be held in any compatible Solana wallet.

Revoked authorities

Both the mint authority (ability to create new tokens) and freeze authority (ability to freeze wallets) for TAIL have been permanently revoked. This removes centralised control risks and is verifiable on-chain.

Transparency is a core principle of TrustTails. We would rather you understand the limits than be misled by a number on a screen.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Honest answers about this tool and crypto token arithmetic in general.

Is this calculator predicting the price of TAIL?

No. The calculator performs arithmetic only: tokens × price you enter = total value. The price field is entirely in your control — you choose the number. We have no way to predict what price a token will trade at, and we would never claim otherwise. TrustTails is pre-launch and not currently traded on any exchange.

Why are there example prices in the scenario table?

The table shows how the arithmetic changes across a range of prices so you can quickly scan multiple scenarios without manually re-entering numbers. The prices shown (e.g. $0.00001, $0.0001, $0.001, $0.01) are common reference points used in educational crypto discussions. They are not targets, forecasts, or recommendations. They exist purely to illustrate how multiplication works at different scales.

What is the total supply of TAIL and why does it matter?

TrustTails (TAIL) has a fixed total supply of 1,000,000,000 tokens. The mint authority has been permanently revoked, which means no additional tokens can ever be created. Knowing the total supply is important because market capitalisation = price × supply. A fixed supply removes the risk of the project team inflating supply and diluting existing holders. You can verify this on-chain at Solscan.

Can I buy TAIL right now?

No. TrustTails is currently pre-launch. TAIL is not listed on any exchange and is not available to purchase. Any person or account claiming to sell TAIL tokens right now is running a scam. Watch the official X account and official Telegram for verified launch announcements. Read the How to Buy guide to prepare.

What does "revoked mint authority" mean and why is it good?

On the Solana network, the "mint authority" is the permission to create new tokens of a given type. When mint authority is revoked, the action is permanent and on-chain — no one, including the project founders, can ever mint additional TAIL tokens. This removes a major trust risk: project teams with active mint authority can inflate supply, diluting existing holders' percentage ownership. TAIL's fixed supply of 1,000,000,000 is therefore truly fixed. You can verify this independently on Solscan.

What is the difference between portfolio value and market cap?

Portfolio value (what this tool calculates) = your tokens × price. It tells you what your specific holding would be worth if a price held.

Market cap = all tokens × price (i.e. total supply × price). For TAIL that would be 1,000,000,000 × price. Market cap is often used to compare projects by scale. Explore the Market Cap Calculator to play with those numbers separately.

Are the currency conversions (AUD, GBP, EUR) accurate?

No — the non-USD figures use approximate, static conversion rates baked into this page for illustrative purposes only. Real exchange rates fluctuate constantly. If you need accurate currency conversions, use a live forex tool. The purpose of the multi-currency display here is simply to give a rough sense of scale for users in non-USD countries.

Is cryptocurrency a good investment?

This website is not qualified to answer that question, and we refuse to pretend otherwise. Cryptocurrency — including TAIL — carries significant risk including the possibility of losing your entire investment. Whether any asset is suitable for you depends on your personal financial situation, risk tolerance, and goals, and those questions require advice from a licensed financial adviser. We present TrustTails factually. We never claim it is a good investment. Always do your own research (DYOR) and only participate with funds you can afford to lose entirely.
