FAQ

Questions, answered straight

What is TrustTails (TAIL)?
A community-first digital token built on Solana with a fixed supply of 1,000,000,000 TAIL and revoked mint & freeze authorities. It begins as a meme coin with an honest, transparency-first roadmap.
Why is the supply fixed?
TAIL has a fixed supply of 1 billion tokens and the mint authority is revoked — so no additional tokens can ever be created. Scarcity is guaranteed by the chain, not by a promise.
Can I buy TAIL right now?
Not yet. The project is in its community-building phase. Launch details will be announced only through our official channels. Ignore anyone who DMs you a "presale" link.
Is this financial advice?
No. Nothing on this website constitutes financial, legal, or investment advice. Cryptocurrency carries significant risk, including total loss of value. Always do your own research.
How do I know it's not a scam?
Don't take our word — verify on-chain. The mint and freeze authorities are revoked and the supply is fixed; you can confirm all of this on the public Solana explorer. See our Is it legit? page for a step-by-step guide.
