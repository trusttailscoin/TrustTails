Our Communication Ethic

Trust & Transparency

We started as a meme to get noticed. Everything after that has to be earned, not claimed. This page is a written record of exactly what we commit to say — and what we refuse to say — at every stage of this project.

The Manifesto

Undersell and Overdeliver

The TrustTails whitepaper is built around one core communication principle: accurate claims build lasting trust, and inflated claims destroy it. Most crypto projects do the opposite — they promise outcomes they cannot control, partner with names they don't have agreements with, and claim audits that don't exist. We think that approach poisons the space. So we chose a different one.

Intentions, Not Promises

Every roadmap item we publish is a stated intention, not a contractual commitment. We will tell you what we are working toward. We will not tell you it is guaranteed.

On-Chain, Not Our Word

The token supply, mint authority revocation, and freeze authority revocation are all permanently verifiable on Solscan. We point you there because the blockchain does not lie — we might make mistakes, the ledger does not.

Earned Reputation

Trust is built over time through consistent, honest communication — not one-time announcements. We expect to be judged on what we do, not what we claim we will do.

Written Commitments

What We Commit To

These are not aspirations. They are operating rules. If we break any of them, the community should call it out — and we will correct it publicly.

Partnerships: Only When Signed

We will announce a partnership only once a signed, public agreement exists. Until that moment, we will describe any discussion as exploratory, not confirmed. The finance-sector direction referenced in our whitepaper is a long-term, unconfirmed ambition — TrustTails currently has no affiliation with, endorsement from, or agreement with any bank, payments network, or financial institution.

Price: We Never Predict It

We will not tell you what TAIL will be worth. We will not suggest a price target, a percentage gain, or a best-case scenario. Nobody controls token price, and claiming otherwise is a form of manipulation. If you see anyone in our name making price predictions, treat it as a scam signal and report it.

Roadmap: Intentions, Not Deadlines

Our roadmap shows the direction we intend to travel. Timeline estimates may shift. Phases may be reordered based on what we learn. We will update it when plans change, and we will explain why — not quietly remove items that did not happen.

Mistakes: Corrected Openly

If we publish something incorrect, we correct it publicly and promptly — not by silently editing a post and hoping no one notices. We will state what was wrong, what is correct, and when the correction was made. Credibility requires that we hold ourselves to this even when it is uncomfortable.

Communication Standards

What Honest Communication Looks Like

The difference between a responsible project and a harmful one often comes down to the language used. Here is exactly where we draw the line.

We will say

  • "We are exploring this direction — no agreement exists yet."
  • "This is an intention on our roadmap, not a confirmed outcome."
  • "Verify the contract address and mint revocation directly on Solscan."
  • "Crypto carries significant risk, including the total loss of value."
  • "We made an error in our earlier post. Here is the correction."
  • "TAIL is pre-launch. It is not yet trading. There is no presale."

We will never say

  • "100x potential" or any price prediction or target.
  • "Partnered with [institution]" before a public agreement is signed.
  • "Audited by [firm]" — we have no current audit to reference.
  • "Buy now before it's too late" or any urgency/FOMO language.
  • "Safe investment" — no cryptocurrency is.
  • "Guaranteed returns" or "guaranteed listing" on any exchange.
Stay Safe

We Will Never DM You First

Because TrustTails is pre-launch and building visibility, scammers impersonate new projects constantly. Here is how to stay safe.

Common Questions

Transparency FAQ

Why won't you give a price prediction?
Because we cannot predict price, and claiming we could would be dishonest. Token price is determined by market supply and demand — neither of which we control once trading begins. Anyone who tells you with confidence what a token will be worth is either guessing or manipulating. We refuse to do either.
What does "intentions, not promises" actually mean for the roadmap?
It means our roadmap reflects the direction we intend to build in, not a legally binding schedule. Phases may shift. Items may be deprioritised based on what we learn. When that happens, we will update the roadmap publicly and explain why — we will not quietly erase what we originally wrote.
You mention finance-sector relevance in the whitepaper. Are you partnered with a bank?
No. The finance-sector direction is a long-term, explicitly unconfirmed ambition described in the whitepaper. TrustTails has no current partnership, affiliation, or agreement with any bank, financial institution, payments network, or regulated entity. We will only announce a partnership once a signed, public agreement exists. Until then, it remains exploratory.
How do I know the mint and freeze authorities are actually revoked?
You do not have to take our word for it. Visit the token page on Solscan, look at the token metadata, and confirm the authority fields yourself. That record is on-chain and immutable. You can also read our Is it legit? page for a full breakdown of what each revocation means.
What happens if TrustTails makes a public mistake?
We correct it openly — not by quietly editing the original post and hoping it goes unnoticed. We will state what was said, what is correct, and when the correction was published. This applies to everything from factual errors in announcements to roadmap changes. Credibility is built through how you handle being wrong, not just how you act when things go right.
Someone DMed me offering TrustTails tokens. Is that real?
No. That is a scam. TrustTails is pre-launch, there is no presale, and our team will never DM you unsolicited to offer tokens or investment opportunities. Block and report the account. Our only official presence is on X, Telegram, and email.
